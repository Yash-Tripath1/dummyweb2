Our Story

Where Tradition
Meets Imagination

The Urban Fork was born from a simple obsession — to honour the soul of Indian cooking while dressing it in something entirely new. We source heirloom spices directly from family farms in Rajasthan and Karnataka, then let our chefs weave them into plates that feel at once deeply familiar and thrillingly unexpected.

Every dish here is a conversation between memory and modernity. Pull up a chair. Let us tell you a story through food.
